Mango Trees

Mango trees are tropical trees that produce mangoes, one of the most popular fruits in the world.

Appearance:

Mango trees have large, shiny leaves and produce small flowers that become mango fruits.

Growing Conditions:

Mango trees grow well in warm, sunny places with good soil.

Uses:

Mangoes can be eaten fresh, made into juice, or used in desserts.

Fun Facts:

  • Mangoes have been grown for over 4,000 years.
  • They are called the King of Fruits.
